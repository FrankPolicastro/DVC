Summary visual aids to provide insightful input into court decisions related to family financial matters
Alimony Scenario Calculator

Calculate how alimony will impact your day-to-day living expenses. Anticipate how an alimony or child support payment could impact day-to-day living expenses and impede savings and retirement.

Using past payment histories from checking and credit card statements, it's possible to anticipate how court-ordered payments could impact cost of living expenses as well as the effects of these payments on savings.
College Tuition Calculator for Divorcees

Calculate college tuition taking into account income and assets. Determine a fair percentage of college tuition responsiblity by analyzing each parent's income and assets.

Make real-time adjustments of each parent's college tuition responsibility by making income, asset, and disparity adjustements from either a notional or percentage perspective.
